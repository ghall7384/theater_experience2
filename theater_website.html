Gracyn's Theater Experience

My name is Gracyn Hall and I have been involved in musical theater for around 7 years. Theater has been a passion of mine ever since my street put on a little neighborhood play for one of our close friends who was fighting cancer at the time. I loved the feeling of being on stage and getting to make others happy. I've learned that musical theater is the art of using acting, movements, and music to tell a story to an audience.
